Fan page of Noelle Mansbridge

Noelle Mansbridge, born June 6, 2003 in Toronto (Canada)
she is a MTF trans music producer and vocalist.

Having gone by multiple aliases online, she finally settled on the moniker "femtanyl", shortly after
in December 2023, she deleted all her old songs under different aliases for mental health reasons.

The music she produces is an extremely distorted version of the "breakcore" genre, defined by many as her own unique genre.
In her songs there is always a little character, called "Token", of an unidentified nature, often represented in
violent and blody scenes, without context, in inexplicable environments, often as if a photo had been taken of a screen.

She mentioned that music was the only escape she had from the synthetic drug of fentanyl,
which she abused of in the past, quoting herself with "i saved my own life with music".
